My Groups And Societies

CodinGame

CodinGame is a website where you can practice your coding skills by playing different games using any language you want. It has all sorts of puzzles, categorised between easy, medium, hard and very hard. The website also has fun competitions, where you can practice your ai building skills by competing against others with your pre coded ai to get the best score, or win a race etc. CodinGame also has a competition called "Clash of Code", where you compete against up to 8 others to either code a challenge the fastest, write the shortest code or reverse engineer an answer as quick as possible. I found that CodinGame is a great way to brush up on my coding skills, and learn how to do things I never thought was possible with code. CodinGame

Thursday Football

Thursday football is an arrangement of 5-a-side football, played with a group of 10 of us every Thursday. It's a friendly, but competitive bit of fun between friends, which also keeps us all fit and communicative. It's all arranged via a whatsapp group, therefore there's no website for the group. However, this weekly event is played at Cotlandswick Leisure Centre, whose website you can find by clicking the link. The match scores are usually high, due to the lack of defensive skill within the group, however, the communication is usually very good between each team.

Top Golf

Top Golf is a driving range, where you can practice your golfing skills, or even just hit some golf balls for some fun, aiming for the 7* different targets on the range. These 7 targets all have different distances, and the size of the targets all depend on their distances. The closer targets being smaller, and the furthest ones being larger. You get 20 balls per game, and can select whatever target you want, or simply hit the ball and hope for the best. There is also a chance to get an extra practice ball, which doesn't count towards your score, and if you get one, you don't know which one it is until you pick the ball up and place it in the ball scanner. Each target has an area you can hit to get double points for your next hit, with the furthest 2 or 3 targets having only double points rings. The 11th ball of each game is a bonus ball. If you get your ball in any of the double points rings on the range, you get a bonus 10 points on this shot only! Also on this shot, if you hit a 10 points ring (which is the bullseye ring only on the furthest 3 targets), you can get a free game! This activity is best played with a group of people to get together and have fun and socialize, or can be played with your family! You get a selection of clubs you can use, from driver all the way down to wedge! If you wanted to practice your putting skills instead, then there's also a fun little mini golf course on site too! Top Golf
*The closest target has 4 targets, all the same distance from the range, and the second closest target has 2, both the same distance from the range
